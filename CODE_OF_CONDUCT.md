# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want Tasks NL to be a welcoming, respectful and constructive project for everyone.

Whether you are reporting a bug, suggesting an idea, improving documentation or contributing code, you are welcome.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Please:

- Be respectful.
- Assume good intentions.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Focus on improving the project.
- Help others learn.
- Be patient with newcomers.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviour is not acceptable:

- Personal attacks.
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Offensive language.
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behaviour.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam or advertising unrelated products.

---

## Project Philosophy

Tasks NL is built around a few simple principles:

- Markdown is the source of truth.
- Keep solutions simple.
- Respect existing users and their data.
- Discuss larger changes before implementing them.
- Small, well-tested improvements are preferred over large rewrites.

---

## Reporting Problems

If you experience unacceptable behaviour, please contact the project maintainer through GitHub.

All reports will be treated respectfully and confidentially.

---

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide or remove comments, discussions, issues or pull requests that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or serious violations may result in blocking further participation.

---

Thank you for helping make Tasks NL a friendly and enjoyable open-source project.
